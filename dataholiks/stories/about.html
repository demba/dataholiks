Our Story

We are a DataHoliks. We are addicted to data. To feed our addiction, we do data science consulting for our clients. Seriously though, our goal is to be your data science experts for hire. We typically focus on small to medium size companies -- usually startups in the retail, connected device, or enterprise software spaces -- but we are open to any clients of all sizes across all industries. We help our clients develop data strategies, mine their data for business intelligence, design production machine learning algorithms, and even implement them in their existing software environment. Our ultimate goal is to solve real world problems -- distill value from data.
