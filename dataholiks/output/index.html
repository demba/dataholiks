    Our Services

    DataHoliks works across the spectrum of data science -- from the initial stages, when a concrete question has yet to be formulated, to the final implementation of production code. Our focus has been on technology startups and midsize businesses because we have found that they derive the most value from targeted data science solutions. Our work is varied, but typically falls into two categories described below. The first impacts your revenue, while the second impacts your product.

    Business Data Analytics

    We use data science to surface insights about your business that affect the bottom line. More importantly, we have extensive experience in helping you leverage the learnings across the different departments in your firm from customer success to sales. We deliver solutions as internal tools, dashboards, detailed reports, and training seminars.

    In the past, we have helped our clients answer questions such as:

    • Who are my most valuable customers and what features of my product matter to them?
    • If I invest engineering resources in developing a feature what is the expected effect on revenue?
    • How can I improve my sales process to better qualify customers?
    • How can I make my marketing more effective through better targeting?
    • Which customers are about to churn and how can I intervene to keep them?
    • How can I segment my customers to better understand their behaviors?
    • How are temporary events, such as weather, affecting my sales?

    Data Product Development

    We develop data-science driven products using state of the art machine learning techniques deployed in the cloud. We collaborate closely with your engineering team to deliver production code that is tested, scalable, and ready for deployment. We have specialized expertise in Internet of Things (IoT) products -- where the data is from sensors interfacing to the physical world. Our team has a deep background in signal processing and wireless communication that is particularly suited to IoT applications because we can design the appropriate split between algorithms running on embedded devices in the field and algorithms running in the cloud.

    In the past, we have developed data products that:

    • Optimize the HVAC start time for a commercial buildings to save energy.
    • Attribute energy consumption in a home to different factors
    • Determine the occupancy of a home from a indoor sensor network.
    • Forecast electricity consumption and demand response load shed on the electrical grid.
    • Optimize irrigation system operation based on soil and weather measurements.
    • Optimally route cars to minimize the time to find street parking.
